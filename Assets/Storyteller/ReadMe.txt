to launch Storyteller go to Tools>DaiMangou>Storyteller

You will see a window, one will be the storyteller and the other will be the Start window . in the start wndow you will find buttons which lead you to tutorials, forums and discussions.

To begin using storyteller.  select a folder where you want to have your story file created, then in the Storyteller , give your story a name , the default name will be "New Story". you will see three buttons the 1st is for games, 2nd for stories, 3rd for storyboards. click the buton which represents the type of project you wish to create.
Then click create.

you will be presented with a blank window.

to begin creating a story , right click on the canvas and go to Make Node> Character Node.

you can then begin adding more nodes which you will find in the Make Node menu.
